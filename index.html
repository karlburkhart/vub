Visualizing the unknown Balkans

    The aim of the Visualizing the unknown Balkans project is turning a spotlight on regions of the Balkans, which are currently under-represented in international archaeology because of the fragmentation of Balkan prehistory in recent decades, through the visualisation of research results of five key areas. OREA researchers and partners from Bosnia and Herzegovina and Serbia present the geographical area from a cross-cultural and diachronic perspective spanning from the Early Neolithic to Late Bronze Age.:

    • First farmers and herders in Leskovac region
    • Bubanj and the rising of Copper Age central places
    • Bronze Age Metallurgy in East Serbia
    • Late Bronze Age communities in Bosnia
    • Prehistoric Burial Tumuli
